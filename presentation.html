Business Context & Target Audience

The Business Problem

Retail operations leadership currently relies on manual, spreadsheet-based reporting that takes days to produce. Three critical blind spots exist:

  • Discount policies are eroding $135K+ in annual profit with no visibility
  • Regional performance varies by 2x (Central: 7.9% vs. West: 14.9% margin) with no root-cause analysis
  • Product-level losses in Tables and Bookcases go undetected, bleeding $21K/year

Target Audience

  • VP of Retail Operations — daily KPI monitoring
  • Regional Managers — performance benchmarking
  • Category Buyers — product profitability decisions
  • Finance Team — discount policy enforcement

Discount Impact on Profit Margin

Critical Discovery: The 20% Discount Cliff

Margins are positive up to 20% discount, then turn sharply negative. Orders above 20% discount generate -$135K in losses — wiping out 47% of total profit. This is the single highest-impact finding in the dataset.

AI-POWERED FEATURES

🤖 What-If Discount Simulator

The dashboard's flagship AI feature. Users adjust a discount cap slider and growth rate, and see in real-time:

  • Current vs. projected profit comparison
  • Exact dollar amount of profit recovered
  • Next-year revenue forecast at chosen growth rate
  • Margin comparison bar chart (before vs. after)

All calculations run against the currently filtered data — combine with Year/Region/Category filters for targeted scenario planning.

Dynamic Predictive Insights

  • Revenue Forecasting — projects next-year revenue from calculated growth rates
  • Risk Detection — auto-surfaces worst sub-category and weakest region
  • Anomaly Alerts — insight bar highlights profit-eroding patterns dynamically

Operational UX Alignment

Conclusion & Next Steps

What We Demonstrated

  • A fully interactive dashboard parsing 9,994 rows of real transactional data in the browser
  • 3 filter dimensions (Year, Region, Category) that recalculate all visuals in real-time
  • 6 chart types chosen for specific analytical purposes — from time-series trends to discount impact analysis
  • Data-driven predictive insights that surface actionable recommendations automatically
  • One-click PDF export preserving the current filtered analysis state

Key Recommendation

Implement a 20% discount cap policy immediately. This single change, identified through the dashboard's analysis, would recover approximately $95K–$135K per year in profit currently being lost to excessive discounting.

Proposed Next Steps

  • Week 1: Present dashboard to VP Operations for feedback and KPI alignment
  • Week 2: Connect to live data source (database or API) replacing static CSV

  • Month 2: Roll out discount policy changes with dashboard monitoring
  • Month 3: Integrate ML-based forecasting and automated alerting
