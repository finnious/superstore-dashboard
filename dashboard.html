Business Value Narrative

The Problem

Leadership lacks real-time visibility into which products, regions, and discount levels are profitable. Manual reporting takes days and misses the discount-driven profit erosion costing $135K+ annually.

The Solution

This interactive BI dashboard provides instant access to KPIs with dynamic filtering by year, region, and category. Every chart and metric recalculates in real-time, enabling data-driven decisions in seconds.

Expected ROI

Implementing the discount cap recommendation alone would recover ~$95K/year. Combined with sub-category optimization and regional interventions, estimated annual profit uplift of $120K–$150K.
